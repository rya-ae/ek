Philippine Indigenous Arts

Weaving

Facing mass-produced competition, efforts are underway to revitalize indigenous weaving by engaging youth.

Wood Carving

Pre-colonial Filipinos carved tools and decor, reflecting tribal hierarchy and celebrating events.

Pottery

Philippine pottery exhibits a rich historical tradition, evolving from functional vessels to intricate art forms across diverse regions.

Wood Carving | The Man Lifting the Barbel

The "Man Lifting a Barbell," a rare wood carving likely created by Jose Rizal, showcases his interest in physical fitness and offers a unique perspective on the national hero beyond his literary works.

Pottery | Manunggul Jar

The Manunggul Jar, a 2,700-year-old burial jar from Palawan, showcases intricate carvings depicting a soul's journey to the afterlife. This masterpiece of Philippine pottery reflects ancient beliefs and artistic mastery.
